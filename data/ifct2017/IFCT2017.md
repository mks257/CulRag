# Indian Food Composition Tables (IFCT) 2017

Citation
--------
Longvah, T., Ananthan, R., Bhaskarachary, K., & Venkaiah, K. (2017). Indian Food Composition Tables 2017. National Institute of Nutrition, Indian Council of Medical Research, Hyderabad, India.

Summary
-------
IFCT 2017 provides laboratory-measured nutrient composition for 528 commonly consumed Indian raw foods across ~151 components (macros, micronutrients, and selected amino acids/fatty acids). Data were sampled regionally and include food names in multiple Indian languages.

Access
------
Official materials and PDFs are available from the National Institute of Nutrition (NIN): https://www.nin.res.in
A machine-readable `ifct2017` package has been published by third parties; verify source and licensing before reuse.

Licensing and Use Notes
-----------------------
- NIN allows reproduction and citation of IFCT data for academic purposes with proper acknowledgement.
- Creating a product or redistributing a compiled/electronic database may require written permission from NIN. Contact NIN for commercial or product uses.

Suggested Acknowledgement
------------------------
"Data source: Indian Food Composition Tables 2017 (Longvah et al., 2017), National Institute of Nutrition, Indian Council of Medical Research, Hyderabad, India."

Notes for CulRAG
----------------
- Use IFCT values as ground-truth for nutritional claims and evaluation.
- When distributing derived datasets, include the citation and a short license note and, if in doubt, request permission from NIN.
