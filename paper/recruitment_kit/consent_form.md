## What is this study about?

We built a website that suggests Indian meals based on your preferences using
artificial intelligence (AI). We want to learn whether the website is easy to
use, whether its suggestions feel culturally appropriate, and how it could be
improved. This is a small pilot study of 8–10 people over 4 weeks.

## What will I be asked to do?

- Use the website at least 3 times a week for 4 weeks to get meal suggestions.
- Rate suggestions you try (thumbs up/down and optional comments).
- Complete a short survey at the start (about 5 minutes), a quick check-in at
  Week 2, and a final survey at Week 4 (about 10 minutes).
- Optionally report your weight at the start, Week 2, and Week 4. **Reporting
  your weight is optional at every point.**

## Important: this is not medical advice

The website's suggestions are informational only. They are generated by an AI
system and are **not** medical, dietetic, or nutritional advice. Do not use
the website to manage a medical condition. If you have any concerns about
your diet or health, consult a qualified professional. The website's allergen
filter is automatic and imperfect — always check ingredients yourself.

## What are the risks?

We believe risks are minimal. The website only suggests common foods; it
never tells you to restrict eating. Possible minor risks are the time
involved and the small chance a suggestion is inaccurate — which is why every
suggestion shows a warning banner if our automated checks found a problem.
If participating raises any distress about food or body image, please stop
and contact us; we will share support resources [RESOURCES TO BE INSERTED].

## What are the benefits?

You may find useful meal ideas, but there is no guaranteed benefit. Your
feedback will help research on culturally aware health technology.

## How will my data be protected?

- You will be identified by a code (like "P07"), not your name. The list
  connecting codes to names is stored separately and only the two researchers
  can access it.
- The website does not collect your name, email, or location.
- Published results will only use combined or anonymized data. If we quote
  your written feedback, we will remove anything identifying, and only if you
  check the box below.
- Data are kept for [X years per institutional policy], then deleted.

## Do I have to participate?

No. Participation is completely voluntary. You can stop at any time, for any
reason, without penalty — just email us. If you withdraw, we will delete your
identifiable data on request.
