# CulRag
CulRAG: Culturally Grounded Retrieval-Augmented Generation  for Personalized Indian Dietary Guidance  A research project building and evaluating a RAG-based AI system  that leverages LLMs and culturally-adapted knowledge bases to provide  personalized nutrition recommendations in Indian dietary context. 

### Research Question
**Can RAG-based systems provide culturally-appropriate and nutritionally-accurate dietary guidance 
when trained on Indian-specific food databases and Ayurvedic principles?**

### Key Innovation
- ✅ First systematic evaluation of **cultural grounding** in RAG nutrition systems
- ✅ RAG system specifically optimized for **Indian cuisine** (800+ foods from IFCT 2017)
- ✅ Integration of **Ayurvedic principles** with modern nutrition science
- ✅ Evaluation of **hallucination rates** and **cultural relevance** in dietary recommendations
